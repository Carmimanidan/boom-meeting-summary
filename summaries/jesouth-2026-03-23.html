EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Overview

Idan walked Janet through a comprehensive demo of Boom's all-in-one property management platform for her 35 owner-operated short-term rental properties across the UK. Janet currently has no PMS in place, making this a greenfield onboarding. The demo covered AI-powered guest communication (handling 80-90% of conversations automatically), a guest portal with digital check-in and upsell capabilities, a sales AI agent achieving 15% higher inquiry conversion, channel management across 250+ platforms, property-level financial tracking with owner P&L statements, and a built-in website builder. Janet expressed strong interest and requested a quote to review before making a decision.
KEY INSIGHTS

Discussion Points

🔍Current Challenges
  • Managing 35 properties with no PMS — everything done manually
  • No unified guest communication system across channels
  • No automated financial tracking or owner reporting
  • Limited visibility into property-level performance and ROI
  • No direct booking channel or website
✅How Boom Addresses This
  • Single all-in-one platform replacing manual workflows — one dashboard for everything
  • AI-powered unified inbox handling WhatsApp, SMS, email, and OTA messages with multilingual support
  • Automated property-level P&L statements with one-click owner report exports
  • Investment KPIs per property: cash-on-cash return, ROI, NOI, occupancy rate
  • Built-in website builder with multi-language/currency support and Airbnb-style booking flow
🚀Growth Opportunities
  • Growing portfolio from 35 to 38 properties — needs scalable operations
  • AI sales agent can boost inquiry conversion by 15%, driving more revenue from existing traffic
  • Guest portal upsells (early/late checkout, marketplace products) to increase ADR
  • 250+ channel distribution to maximize occupancy across the UK market
  • Dynamic pricing tools to optimize rates based on market data
