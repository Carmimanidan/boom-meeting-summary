  • Too many listings to price manually, but too few to justify a dedicated revenue manager
  • Guest communication volume growing faster than your team — response times slipping during peak periods
  • Cleaning and maintenance coordination getting complex as the portfolio grows
  • Multiple tools for different functions — no single source of truth for your operations
  • Scaling to 150+ properties feels impossible without doubling your team
✅How Boom Solves This
  • Dynamic pricing engine — AI sets optimal rates across all 99 listings based on demand, competition, and seasonality
