KEY INSIGHTS

Discussion Points

🔍Current Pain Points
  • No PMS — managing 8 active listings directly through Airbnb with no backend visibility for owners
  • Tried Hostaway and Price Labs but found both painful and difficult despite highly qualified team
  • Owners asking for backend access to bookings and rates — no way to provide it currently
  • Dynamic pricing uncertainty — unsure how to adjust for winter vs. summer seasons
  • Complex maintenance workflows (e.g., sourcing Italian toilet parts, contractor coordination) handled manually
✅How Boom Solves This
  • All-in-one platform replacing Hostaway, Price Labs, and 6-7 other standalone tools under one $29/property/month subscription
  • AI-powered guest communications in any language (80-90% automated), with smart escalation for sensitive topics
  • Built-in dynamic pricing engine — no separate subscription needed, handles seasonal adjustments automatically
  • Owner portal with real-time P&L, investment KPIs (ROI, NOI, cap rate), calendar visibility, and maintenance documentation
  • End-to-end maintenance workflow: task creation, contractor coordination, before/after photos — all automated
🚀Growth Enablers
  • AI sales agent improves inquiry conversion by 15% with automated follow-ups and price negotiation
  • Upsell store for add-ons (early check-in, late checkout, pool heater) — one Boom client generated $60K in add-on revenue in 90 days
  • Orphan night detection automatically offers discounts to fill inventory gaps
  • Exclusive high-level properties can be managed alongside Airbnb with shared calendar functionality
  • Boom's deep South African market expertise makes onboarding seamless — familiar with all local regulations
